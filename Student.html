About College

Gossner College was established in November, 1971 by our Founder Principal Rt. Reverend Dr. Nirmal Minz. Gossner College is a Minority College, affiliated to Ranchi University and is managed by Gossner Evangelical Lutheran Church (GELC) of Chotanagpur and Assam. Read more..
